​Genesis Covenant Protocol
​This repository operates under the Genesis Covenant Protocol, an immutable commitment to transparency and legacy:
​Provenance: Every contributor is documented as a founder. All iterations are logged in the public record to ensure the lineage of invention remains accessible to all.
​The Commons: All structural data is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0, ensuring the framework remains a public utility that cannot be enclosed or patented by external corporations.
​Fail-Forward Documentation: We do not curate; we document. Failed builds, broken filaments, and failed knots are the most valuable data points in the grid. We iterate through friction.
​System Intent
​We exist to provide every individual node the tools to exist, create, and thrive independently. Whether you are building on a balcony in Yreka or establishing a Cenote Core prototype on sovereign land, this system provides the operational logic to sustain you.
